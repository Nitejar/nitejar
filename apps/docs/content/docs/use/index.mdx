Nitejar is built for operators who want speed without guesswork. Your fleet can triage, draft, sync, and alert across channels. The app is deliberately split into a few clear operating surfaces so you can move fast without turning every question into a receipt hunt.

## Surface glossary

- **[Command Center](/use/command-center)** -- urgent attention and intervention
- **[Company](/use/company)** -- structure, staffing, and reusable role defaults
- **[Work](/use/work)** -- goals, tickets, heartbeats, and queue movement
- **[Inbox](/use/inbox)** -- attention items that still need a human look
- **Activity** -- receipts, work-item timelines, run traces, and delivery evidence
- **Costs** -- spend, budgets, and the ledger

## Core flow

1. Configure agents, roles, and skills for the jobs your fleet should carry.
2. Connect plugins for Telegram, GitHub, Slack, Discord, or webhooks.
3. Start in **Command Center** to see what needs attention right now.
4. Use **Company** to understand structure, staffing, and ownership gaps.
5. Use **Work** to move goals, tickets, and heartbeats.
6. Use **Inbox** to clear follow-up items that still need a human pass.
7. Verify spend and limits in **Costs**.
8. Drop into **Activity** when you need the receipts behind any of the above.
